About

I am a hobby photographer drawn to the quiet in-between moments — the last light on a ridgeline, a city exhaling at dusk, the improbable detail of something very small.

This site collects my work across landscape, cityscape, macro, wildlife, travel and family photography. Everything here was made for the love of looking closely.
